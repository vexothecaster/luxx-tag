About the Game

luxx tag: The Wild Escape is a fan-made VR game where you must escape from a jungle filled with dangerous hunters. Swing your arms to navigate through a vast open-world environment filled with challenges and dangers. Team up with friends or face off against others in a thrilling pursuit for freedom.

Game Features

Dynamic Movement

Experience unique locomotion by swinging your arms to move through the jungle in a thrilling and engaging way.

Challenging Hunters

Face off against intelligent hunters who use the environment and their skills to track you down. Will you escape?

Multiplayer Action

Team up with friends or compete with others in adrenaline-pumping modes that require strategy and agility.
